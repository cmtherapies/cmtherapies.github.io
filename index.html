About

An evidenced-based, queer-affirming, and trauma-informed approach to massage


Dedicated to providing an inclusive healing experience to relieve stress, manage pain, and feel comfortable in your body, I believe all bodies deserve to be nurtured. My journey with massage therapy began at the American Institute of Alternative Medicine in 2016, following a career as a dancer and puppeteer. I earned my Bachelor of Arts in Dance from Agnes Scott College, where massage was an invaluable element of caring for myself. Seeking a deeper understanding of human behavior and the dynamics of social interaction, I furthered my education at Case Western Reserve University, obtaining my Master of Science in Social Work. This invaluable experience equipped me with the tools and compassion to navigate the complexities of the human experience. Combining my skills in movement, touch therapy, and social work, I am deeply committed to facilitating healing and transformation in others.
