About

An evidenced-based, queer-affirming, and trauma-informed approach to massage


Dedicated to providing an inclusive healing experience to relieve stress, manage pain, and feel comfortable in your body, I believe all bodies deserve to be nurtured. I began my massage career in 2016 at the American Institute of Alternative Medicine. Growing up as a dancer, massage was an invaluable part of caring for myself. I received my Bachelor of Arts in Dance from Agnes Scott College and performed as a dancer and puppeteer. My experience in medical massage led me to Case Western Reserve University, where I received my Master of Science in Social Work.
